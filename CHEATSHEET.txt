DATABASE
-----------------------------------------------------------
  PostgreSQL database: allotment_research

  Main tables:
    federal_register_claims   10,976 rows  FR claims
    blm_allotment_patents    239,845 rows  BLM patents
    forced_fee_patents_rails  17,560 rows  Claim-patent links
    trust_fee_linkages        29,229 rows  Trust-to-fee conversions
    wilson_table_vi              212 rows  1934 reservation data
    wilson_annual_sales          ~32 rows  Annual sales 1903-1934
    murray_comparative            52 rows  1947 vs 1957 by agency
    murray_transactions          520 rows  Transactions by agency/year
    murray_trust_removal          83 rows  Removal by area office/year
    murray_agency_removal         41 rows  Total removal by agency
    murray_lands_acquired         23 rows  Lands acquired since 1930
