Trust → Fee Linkages

Trust-to-fee patent conversions recovered from BLM patent records by two independent passes: remarks-regex (cross-references like SEE SERIAL PATENT NR 75921-09 FOR FEE PATENT parsed from the patent remarks) and parcel-matching (same PLSS parcel + same allottee name family, used when the remarks are absent or contain a transcription typo). Both layers live in trust_fee_linkages_recovered with a source column distinguishing them. Distinct from the older trust_fee_linkages table built by allotment-number matching.
