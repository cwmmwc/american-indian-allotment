Federal Register Claims Search

Search 35,686 claims published in the Federal Register on March 31 and November 7, 1983, including forced fee patents, secretarial transfers, unapproved land sales, tax forfeitures, trespass, and other claim types.

These claims come from two 1983 Federal Register publications documenting injuries to Indian allottees and their heirs. Claim types include forced fee patents (land converted to fee status without consent), secretarial transfers, unapproved land sales, tax forfeitures, trespass, timber removal, and more. Use the Claim Type filter to explore specific categories.

Claims are organized by BIA agency code — an alphanumeric identifier from the Federal Register that encodes the BIA area office and local agency. The first letter indicates the area office (A = Aberdeen, B = Anadarko, C = Billings, F = Minneapolis, G = Muskogee, H = Phoenix, J = Sacramento, N = Navajo, P = Portland). Case numbers are sequential within each agency code, so case #001 under A00007 (Flandreau) is a different claim from #001 under C51201 (Blackfeet). Read more
