Data Portraits of Indian Land Loss

Experimental visualizations inspired by W.E.B. Du Bois's data portraits for the 1900 Paris Exposition. Du Bois used bold color, unconventional form, and declarative composition to make statistical evidence speak to injustice. These plates attempt the same for the allotment era.
